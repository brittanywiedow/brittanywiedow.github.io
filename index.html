Bio

I began work as a commission-based artist in traditional mediums (watercolor, pastel, and ink).
At the same time, I volunteered my free time to various animal shelters and advocacy groups and did graphic art for websites, social media, products, fundraisers and charity projects.
Now, I am a freelance graphic artist, and my current focus is on business start up needs. I also spend some of my time working on concept art for games.
